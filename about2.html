About City Of Refugee Assembly

Discover our story, our vision, and the heart behind our ministry.

Our History

City Of Refugee Assembly was founded with a burden to provide a spiritual home for people from all walks of life. Inspired by Psalm 46:1, we began as a small fellowship and have grown into a vibrant church family committed to worship, discipleship, and outreach.

Our Vision

To know Christ intimately and to make Him known globally. We envision a Spirit-empowered church that transforms individuals, families, and communities for the glory of God.

Our Mission

Reach the lost with the Gospel, teach believers through discipleship, and send them into the world as ambassadors of Christ. We are committed to holistic ministry that touches every part of society.

Our Core Beliefs

The Bible

We believe the Bible is the inspired, infallible Word of God, the final authority for faith and life.

The Trinity

We believe in one God revealed in three persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit.

Salvation

We believe salvation comes by grace through faith in Jesus Christ alone.

Prayer & Worship

We believe prayer and worship are vital for a growing relationship with God and the advancement of His kingdom.

Community

We believe in the power of authentic community and fellowship as the body of Christ.

Excellence

We pursue excellence in all we do as a reflection of God’s glory and worthiness.

Our Leadership

Community Impact

We are more than a church service — we are a movement of believers impacting society. Through education, outreach programs, and partnerships, we meet practical needs while sharing the love of Christ.

Outreach

Food drives, hospital visits, evangelism, and compassionate ministries that touch lives directly.

Education

Scholarships, mentorship, and training programs equipping the next generation for leadership.
