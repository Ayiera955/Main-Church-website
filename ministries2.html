Our Ministries

Empowering youth with mentorship, leadership programs, and spiritual growth opportunities to prepare them for a Christ-centered life.

Leading the congregation in worship through song, instruments, and the arts – glorifying God with excellence and passion.

Building strong, godly men through fellowship, mentorship, and outreach to impact families and communities positively.

Empowering women through mentorship, prayer, and service as they grow in their faith and impact their families and society.

Reaching out with love to the less fortunate through charity, evangelism, hospital visits, and education initiatives.

Supporting families through counseling, marriage enrichment, and pastoral care to nurture strong and healthy homes.
