About Us

We are a Bible-believing church passionate about worship, discipleship, and community transformation. Our assignment is to make Christ known and to equip believers for works of service.

Vision

To Know Christ & Make Him Known

We envision a vibrant, Spirit-led church raising Christ-like disciples who influence culture with the Gospel.

Mission

Reach • Teach • Send

We reach the lost, teach the found, and send equipped believers to serve in the church and in the marketplace.

Core Values

Word • Prayer • Worship • Excellence • Community

We build everything on Scripture, prayer, and Christ-exalting worship while pursuing excellence and authentic community.
